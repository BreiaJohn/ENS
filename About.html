About Me

My name is Breia, I am originally from Syracuse, NY. I went to school for early childhood education as well as health sciences. My goal was to become a mid wife. Life happened and I ended up starting a family of my own. Fast forward I now have 4 beautiful children. While I was giving birth to my second child I realized I wanted to work with babies. The mid wife that was assisting me pushed me at my most painful point and in the end I was just so grateful to have had her. So from that point on I knew what my mission in life was to do.
